Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world.
It is played between two teams of eleven players each.
The game is played with a bat, a ball, and wickets.
Cricket originated in England and later spread worldwide.
The main formats of cricket are Test, One Day International (ODI), and T20.
A batsman scores runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets.
Bowlers try to dismiss batsmen and restrict runs.
Fielders support the bowlers by catching the ball and preventing runs.
The International Cricket Council (ICC) governs international cricket.
Cricket teaches teamwork, discipline, strategy, and sportsmanship.
